How can I subscribe to the Newsletter? There are just 4 easy steps to subscribe:
1. Enter your email address into the "email address" field.
2. Select the format in which you wish to receive your Newsletter: "HTML" (with pictures) or "Text" (Text only).
3. Select your geographical locations and your preferred store. This ensures that you receive the special offers that are available in your favourite store. You also have the option to register with your full name and postal address.
4. Click on "Subscribe". A confirmation email will then be sent to your email address. In order to receive your Newsletters, you must then click on the link in this confirmation email to activate your subscription.
If you wish to change any of your details at any time, (e.g. your favourite store) please use the appropriate link in your newsletter.

What is the distinction between Lidl and other Discounters? Lidl has a simple philosophy – offering customers top quality products at the lowest possible prices. In achieving this, Lidl seeks to keep overheads to a minimum and eliminate any unnecessary costs. Savings are then passed directly on to shoppers, who can enjoy huge price differences from those in other supermarkets. In addition to stocking its own top quality range of products, Lidl also features big household brands like Coca Cola, Heinz, Cadbury and Weetabix to name just a few, so you can get what you want, at the prices you want. Don’t forget to keep an eye on Lidl’s legendary weekly food and non-food specials for extra special value for money!
In addition to Lidl’s commitment to great value, Ireland’s favourite discount retailer places huge emphasis on quality, freshness and availability as well as local sourcing of products where possible.
We aim to make your shopping experience with us as pleasurable as possible and endeavour to offer a friendly and fast service, in easily laid out, well stocked stores that offer free car parking to all of its customers.
With over ten years of retailing under our belt in both Ireland and Northern Ireland, we are firmly committed to continue our promise of offering superb quality at fantastic value for money! We look forward to see you soon!

What are Cookies and what are they used for on this website? To make your visit on our website as comfortable and enjoyable as possible, Lidl Ireland uses cookies. Cookies are small pieces of information sent by a particular web server to a web browser, which enables the server to collect information from the browser. It enables your computer’s server to access information on our website faster and more efficiently.

Is my personal data collected when I visit the homepage?  When you visit http://www.lidl.ie/ we do not collect your personal data! However, in order to keep your visits as pleasant as possible, the homepage is Cookie enabled.

Lidl prides itself on offering freshness and availability at all times and adheres to the strictest of EU guidelines with regard to the production and regulation of its products. This means you can have complete piece of mind that you buy not only at great value, but that you will enjoy delicious, unrivalled quality products too.
